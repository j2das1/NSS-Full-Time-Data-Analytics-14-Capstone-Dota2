Info for group stage matches in TI2016:

All matches were played in a best of two:
	-A win (2-0) provided 2 points
	-A draw (1-1) provided 1 point
	-A loss (0-2) provided 0 points

Top 4 teams in each group advances to upper bracket of the main event
Bottom 4 teams in each group advances to lower bracket of the main event
